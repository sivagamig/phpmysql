FABRICS

WOVENFABRICS

Guhan Apparel has gained lot of insights on what it takes to offer the best of Woven Fabrics. Woven fabrics at Guhan Apparel reflect the crisscross patterns that are created when all tiny fibers are woven together. Guhan Apparel offers a wide range of woven fabrics with abundance of care offered in attaining smooth edges in each product. Guhan Apparel offers woven fabric in 100% cotton, cotton / lycra and manmade yarn.

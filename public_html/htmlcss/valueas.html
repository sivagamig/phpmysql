Guhanapprels offers intense focus on placing its process and systems around the value adding eco system through printing and wash effects.

PRINTING

If Fabric reflects the body of apparel then it is obvious that printing is the core soul of the apparel. And yes Guhan Apparel offers extensive priority towards fine tuning its printing infrastructure. Highly competent human resource gaining access to advanced printing technologies is the ultimate recipe for a quality printing and we Guhan Apparel at understand and adopt it very well in to practice.

The printing techniques and inks which we use are accepted to be the best by our clients. Some of the few techniques are

  • Plastisol

  • Pigment

  • Discharge

  • Dye Sublimation

  • Transfer Stickers and much more

Guhan Apparel has strong in house process in its operations that encourages waste reduction and encourages the act of recycling there by contributing to the culture of eco friendly business practices. Stringent surveillance and competent training mechanisms to human resources are some of our core strengths at Guhan Apparel.

Our machinery configurations include the,

  • Specially designed Softwares

  • HP plotter for large format printing

  • Image setter for high end positive film

  • 6-Kw screen exposing machines

  • High pressure water gun

  • MHM Automatic screen Printers

  • Imported curing Machine

  • Fusing Machines.

WASH EFFECTS

SPECIAL WASH

Special Washes For Knitted, Woven and DENIM garments

  • DENIM WASH – Gives faded effect on the the garments

  • INDIGO WASH – Fixes and reduces denim color.

  • STONE WASH – gives the faded look to the new cloth.

  • GOLF BALL WASH – Gives wash down effect and smoothness.

  • BIO POLISHING – removes pills and gives good hand feel.

  • SILICON WASH – Gives bouncy and smooth surface.

  • ANTI MICROBIAL – Resists bacteria, Fungus and germs.

  • ANTIQUE WASH – Old used wash effect.

  • TEFLON WASH – Repels water, oil and stains.

  • HYDROPHILIC FINISH – Improves wicking pro. and good hand feel.

  • SOIL RELEASE FINISH – Releases the soil easily from the garment.

  • ASH FINISH – Anti static, Soil release, Hydrophilic.

  • FRAGRANCE FINISH – Gives perfumes smell.

  • ELASTOMERIC FINISH – Increases the elasticity.

  • UV PROTECTION FINISH – Resists UV rays and protects.

  • ANTI OZONE FINISH – Resists ozone rays and protects.

  • ACID WASH – Give worn effect and fads the color.

  • OCEAN MAGIC FINISH – Gives worn effect and fads the face side of the fabric.

  • SNOW WASH – Oxidizes the indigo and gives snow effect.

About Guhan

Guhan Apparel has its origin at Coimbatore - a city that is highly renowned as Textile city of south India. Guhan Apparel had its inception in 2016 with the team of professionals with a industry oriented experience for more than a decade. Guhan Apparel has been able to manufacture and Design T shirts on a large scale there by accomplishing volumes of 10,000 + pieces of products to its customer base.

We intend to contribute to the competitive position of our clients through means of offering quality products to them. At Guhan Apparel we understand the complexities involved in establishing a brand new clothing tag or in developing existing apparel brands. It is evident that unique selling proposition is the core ingredient that differentiates one brand from that of the other. There have been cases where the buyer rejects the product due to higher pricing and at Guhan Apparel we make no compromise on brand’s fit as well as design attributes and this is the reason why we are here for the customers now.

Guhan Apparel makes sure that clients are made more competitive in the highly competitive T shirt industry in terms of both quality and economical pricing. Through means of partnering with us several firms have been able to gain competitive advantage over their rivals.

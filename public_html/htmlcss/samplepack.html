Sample Pack

Since Guhan Apparel offers extensive composite materials across an array of styles ordering one or more of sample packs would be the best way to assess which suits the best for your use.

At Guhan Apparel we understand the expectations of the customer in terms of getting a real feel of the fabric. Our sample pack comprises variety of fabrics with minimum order quantity that exactly resembles the quality of products that we deliver to you.

We at Guhan Apparel establish process and systems that will aid in gaining trust and confidence amongst our clients. Working on huge lots for clients has complexities such as increased investment volume and timeline. Hence we arrived at an innovative solution for this through introduction of “Sample Packs”.

Sample pack comprise a pack of 20 to 40 samples apparels with a single style or colour, which could be customized as per the expectations and specifications of our clients. Sample packs come with a very minimal charge for the clients so that they can gain insight in to the quality of products and our craftsmanship. Also our sample pack would comprise free air shipping and delivery to the customers door step.
